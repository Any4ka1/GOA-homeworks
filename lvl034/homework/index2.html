The Apothecary Diaries

About the Series

The Apothecary Diaries follows Maomao, a curious and intelligent young woman who is taken to serve in the imperial palace. Using her knowledge of medicine and poisons, she unravels mysterious illnesses and palace intrigues.

Main Characters

  • Maomao — a skilled apothecary with sharp observation and curiosity.
  • Jinshi — a beautiful and enigmatic imperial official.
  • Gyokuyou — a gentle concubine favored by the emperor.

Where to Watch

The anime adaptation is available on major streaming platforms such as Crunchyroll. The manga and light novel are licensed in English by various publishers.
