About the Cartoon

Introduction

Tom and Jerry is an American animated series of short films created in 1940 by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera.

It centers on a rivalry between the title characters Tom, a cat, and Jerry, a mouse.

Main Characters

Tom

Tom is a grey and white domestic short haired cat. He is usually but not always, trying to catch Jerry.

Jerry

Jerry is a small brown house mouse who always lives close to Tom. He is very clever and often outwits Tom.
